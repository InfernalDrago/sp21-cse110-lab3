April 9th, 2021

On Friday April 9th, I met up with my CSE110 group for an Intro Meeting at 5:00 pm. We all introduced ourselves, decided our team name and logo, and played skribbl.io for about an hour. At about 6:00 pm, I met up with the team responsible for organizing this years DataHacks event. We discussed how the event would start off and any last minute news. At 10:30 pm, my partner for the event and I presented our dataset track to the participants and ended our night there.

April 10th, 2021

On Saturday April 10th, I had signed up for two 2 hour sessions when I would have to stay on a voice channel for DataHacks in case any participants had questions concerning the datasets. There weren't many participants in those 4 hours so I passed the time playing video games while waiting for the next person to join the voice channel. With the few participants I interacted with, some of them had questions I didn't understand so I was really stressed with those until another mentor joined in. In between the two sessions I hosted a workshop on writing the final reports that would be turned in to the judges. I presented twice in my time slot because some people arrived late to the session. After my last mentor session ended at 9:00 pm, I watched 28 Days Later with my family and went to bed.

Recording of my Report Writing Workshop I cringe at the sound of my own voice over a recording.
